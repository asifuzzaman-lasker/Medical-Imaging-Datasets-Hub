# 🧠 Brain Medical Imaging Datasets Hub

This repository provides **authentic, research-grade brain MRI and CT imaging datasets** suitable for **deep learning, classification, segmentation, and medical AI research**.

These datasets are widely used in **peer-reviewed papers, MICCAI challenges, and clinical AI development**.

---

# 📊 Dataset List

| Dataset Name | Disease / Purpose | Modalities | Size | Access |
|-------------|------------------|------------|------|--------|
| BraTS | Brain Tumor Segmentation (Glioma) | T1, T1c, T2, FLAIR MRI | 2000+ patients | Public |
| TCIA Brain Collections | Brain Tumor, Cancer | MRI, CT | 30+ collections | Public |
| OpenNeuro | General Brain Imaging | MRI, fMRI, DTI | 74,000+ subjects | Public |
| OASIS | Alzheimer’s Disease | MRI | 2000+ subjects | Public |

# 🧠 1. BraTS (Brain Tumor Segmentation)

**Official Website:**  
https://www.med.upenn.edu/cbica/brats2021/data.html

**Description**
- Gold-standard dataset for brain tumor research
- Used in MICCAI BraTS Challenge
- Includes expert segmentation masks

**Modalities**
- T1
- T1 contrast (T1c)
- T2
- FLAIR

**Tasks**
- Tumor classification
- Tumor segmentation
- Survival prediction

---

# 🧠 2. TCIA (The Cancer Imaging Archive)

**Website:**  
https://www.cancerimagingarchive.net/

**Description**
- NIH-supported medical imaging archive
- Multiple brain tumor datasets

**Format**
- DICOM
- NIfTI

**Tasks**
- Radiomics
- Tumor classification
- Segmentation

---

# 🧠 3. OpenNeuro

**Website:**  
https://openneuro.org/

**Description**
- One of the largest open brain imaging repositories
- Includes healthy and diseased brain scans

**Modalities**
- MRI
- fMRI
- Diffusion MRI

**Best for**
- Pretraining foundation models
- Self-supervised learning

---
