# NIH Chest X-ray14 Dataset

**Description:**  
Public chest X-ray dataset released by the National Institutes of Health (NIH), containing 112,120 frontal-view X-ray images.

- **Modality:** Chest X-ray
- **Task:** Multi-label classification
- **Size:** 112,120 images
- **Classes:** 14 thoracic diseases
- **Source:** https://nihcc.app.box.com/v/ChestXray-NIHCC
