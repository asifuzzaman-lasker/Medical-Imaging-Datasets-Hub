# CheXpert – Large Chest X-ray Dataset

**Description:**  
CheXpert is a large dataset of chest radiographs from Stanford University with uncertainty labels for 14 thoracic diseases.

- **Modality:** Chest X-ray
- **Task:** Multi-label classification
- **Size:** 224,000+ images
- **Labels:** 14 thoracic observations
- **Source:** https://stanfordmlgroup.github.io/competitions/chexpert/
- **License:** Research use
